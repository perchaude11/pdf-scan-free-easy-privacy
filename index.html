Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-28

This Privacy Policy explains how PDF Scan Free & Easy ("the app") handles information when you use it.

What the app does

The app lets you capture or import images, apply enhancements, and export them as PDF files. Processing happens on your device.

Information the app handles

  • Camera access to capture documents (only when you choose to scan).
  • Photos and files you select for scanning (processed locally).
  • Generated PDF files saved to your device at your request.

Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or receive information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, and ad interaction data to provide and measure ads. This data is handled by Google according to their policies.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data related to advertising is processed by Google AdMob as described above.

Data retention

The app does not maintain a user account or store your scanned documents on a server. Files remain on your device unless you delete them.

Children's privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest version.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the developer contact listed on the Google Play Store listing for the app.
